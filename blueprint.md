# Project Blueprint

## Overview

**Outfyt AI** is a complete wardrobe styling assistant that helps you manage your clothes, get daily outfit recommendations, and discover new styles. It's a progressive web app (PWA) built with modern, framework-less web technologies.

## Implemented Features

*   **Onboarding:** A multi-step process to personalize the user's experience.
*   **Navigation:** A bottom navigation bar with the following items: Home, Wardrobe, Events, Mimic, Insights, and Premium.
*   **Wardrobe Management:**
    *   Add, edit, and delete clothing items.
    *   Upload images of your clothes from your device or take a picture with the camera.
    *   Mark items as favorites or in the laundry.
    *   View your entire wardrobe in a grid.
*   **Daily Styling:**
    *   Get a personalized "Outfit of the Day" based on your style preferences and the weather.
    *   Regenerate outfits if you don't like the suggestion.
    *   View saved outfits.
*   **Mimic a Look:** Upload an image to get a similar outfit from your wardrobe.
*   **Subscription Model:**
    *   A free tier with basic features.
    *   A premium tier with advanced features like unlimited outfit regenerations and event-based styling.
*   **Responsive Design:** The app is fully responsive and works on all devices.
*   **PWA:** The app is a Progressive Web App, which means it can be "installed" on your device for an app-like experience.
